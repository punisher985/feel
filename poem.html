You are like , a bubble to me.
Bubble of joy indeed.
I know I am being selfish,
but I like that sparkling bubble much,
rather than that dull one.
I hate those things,
things that make you upset.
Cuz they pave the way,
for my sadness too.


You are so real, you are so fun;
That you remind me of, the setting sun.
Yes the setting sun.
Your arms provide me the same calmness,
as if I was at sea shore with you;
Watching the sun uniting with sea,
the same way that our souls have done,
few years ago.
Your breath provides me with same warmness,
as that sunset provides.
and I just don't want the moment to end,
even for my entire life.


I see your eyes, so soothing,
as if the sea is at its best behaviour;
I see depth in those eyes,ofcourse not lies,
but utter respect for me,
those beautiful eyes,
really mesmerize me,
So pretty as can be;
I fell for them way too easily.


When I gaze deeply into you, I try,
I try hard, not to cry.
Your heart so pure for that I would die;
I hear your heartbeats, and mine too,
as if they are trying to put a jingle together.
Jingle that I want to hear from dusk to dawn,
repeating itself on and on.

I tell myself not to look,when you walk on by;
But I take a peek,And so stupidly I smile.


Taking things slow would be best,
as I don't want things to be a mess,
I to live every moment as long as I could,
and keep it with one of our souvenisr,
that we have created with our love so true.


Sitting on that sand,
with sea kissing our feets.
Being in our own Dreamland;
Playing with each others hands and hair,
Too shy to kiss,
I bet we are a wonderful pair.
